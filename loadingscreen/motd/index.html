Joining the Staff Team

What we look for

  • Calm communication under pressure.
  • Consistency — being present beats being loud.
  • Fair judgement and the ability to de-escalate.
  • Basic knowledge of server rules and roleplay standards.

How to apply

  1. Join Discord and open a Staff Application ticket.
  2. Include your age (or confirm you meet the minimum requirement set in Discord), timezone, and availability.
  3. List any prior staff experience (if any) — honesty preferred over bravado.
  4. Explain why you want to help the community, and what you’re good at.

Expectations

  • Staff are role models: no power trips, no favourites.
  • Use evidence and logs — not vibes.
  • Escalate issues properly. If in doubt, ask a senior.
  • Confidential matters stay confidential.
Practical note
Being staff is not a shortcut to rank or loot. It’s work — and we do rather like people who can do it without theatrics.

Quick Rules

  1. Respect all players and staff.
  2. No cheating, exploiting, or evasion.
  3. Keep roleplay serious where required.
  4. Follow NLR/RDM rules as per the server handbook.
  5. Use voice/chat appropriately; no harassment.

Full handbook and detailed rules are provided on Discord.

Support
